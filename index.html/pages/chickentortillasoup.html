Chicken Tortilla Soup

okay so first things first gather these ingrediants!

  • shredded chicken
  • paprika
  • chili powder
  • rice
  • el pato chili tomato sauce
  • tortilla strips
  • salt

once you've gathered these ingredients by any means follow these steps!

  1. boil the shredded chicken
  2. add the rice to a small pot and start frying it
  3. when the rice is golden add the el pato sauce which should be half a cup. for every 1 cup of rice add 1 cup of fluids so had another 1 and a half cups of water
  4. start frying the tortilla chips!
  5. add the seasonings to the chicken to taste
  6. once it's the rice is done its all good to go!
