Spicy Honey Chicken Curry Recipe!

first and foremost! you must gather the following!:

  • 1.5 lbs of chicken
  • 1 whole onion
  • Curry Powder...DUHHHH
  • 2 cups of rice
  • fresh cilantro
  • honey
  • paprika
  • dried chili flakes
  • water
  • cream cheese

So first you must cook the onions until they're see through! then you gotta add the chicken while you start the rice. Once the chicken has cooked almost fully through you add the curry powder and the paprika. also add some water! Before the seasonings burn add the honey and turn it down! Add the cream chease and stir then let it simmer,

tadaaaa all done!
